## 1. 邀请信 (Invitation Letter)

**适用场景：** 邀请外教做评委、邀请同学参加比赛、邀请专家出席会议。

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \text{Dear } \underline{\hspace{4cm}}, \\
\\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [1. \text{写作目的}]: \text{It is with great honor and pleasure for me to invite you to } \\
& \textit{attend / participate in / be a judge for} \text{ the } \underline{\hspace{5cm}} \text{ (活动名称).} \\
\\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [2. \text{过渡句}]: \text{Relevant details go as follows.} \\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [3. \text{细节一：时间地点}]: \text{First, this activity will be held at } \underline{\hspace{2cm}} \text{ (时间) on } \\
& \underline{\hspace{2cm}} \text{ (日期) in } \underline{\hspace{2cm}} \text{ (地点). We hope to take advantage of this} \\
& \text{opportunity to } \underline{\hspace{5cm}} \text{ (目的: e.g., cultivate students' interest).} \\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [4. \text{细节二：活动规模}]: \text{Second, by then, there will be about } \underline{\hspace{1cm}} \text{ students and} \\
& \text{teachers attending this activity.} \\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [5. \text{细节三：活动形式}]: \text{Third, it has also been an online livestreaming, or you might} \\
& \text{be asked to } \underline{\hspace{5cm}} \text{ (e.g., give a speech).} \\
\\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [6. \text{结尾期待}]: \text{I will be rather appreciative if you could spare time to join us.} \\
& \text{Looking forward to your early reply at your earliest convenience.} \\
\\
& \text{Yours,} \\
& \text{Li Ming}
\end{aligned}
$$

## 2. 建议信 (Suggestion Letter)

**适用场景：** 给图书馆提意见、给朋友/学弟学妹提学习建议。

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \text{Dear } \underline{\hspace{4cm}}, \\
\\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [1. \text{身份+目的}]: \text{As a } \underline{\hspace{3cm}} \text{ (身份: student / friend), I am writing} \\
& \text{to offer you some } \textit{conducive / constructive} \text{ suggestions on } \underline{\hspace{4cm}} \text{.} \\
\\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [2. \text{过渡句}]: \text{Relevant suggestions go as follows.} \\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [3. \text{建议一：必做之事}]: \text{First, it would be a must for you to } \underline{\hspace{4cm}} \text{ (动作),} \\
& \text{so that you can } \underline{\hspace{4cm}} \text{ (效果).} \\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [4. \text{建议二：具体做法}]: \text{Second, you had better } \underline{\hspace{4cm}} \text{ (动作). For example,} \\
& \text{you could download an App to } \underline{\hspace{4cm}} \text{ (e.g., enlarge vocabulary).} \\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [5. \text{建议三：通用建议}]: \text{Third, try to } \underline{\hspace{5cm}} \text{ (动作) to polish your } \\
& \underline{\hspace{3cm}} \text{ (技能).} \\
\\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [6. \text{结尾期待}]: \text{Once again, I hope you can accept my tips. Please feel free to reach} \\
& \text{me if you have further questions.} \\
\\
& \text{Yours,} \\
& \text{Li Ming}
\end{aligned}
$$

## 3. 投诉信 (Complaint Letter)

**适用场景：** 电子产品质量差、食品变质、服务态度恶劣。

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \text{Dear Sir or Madam,} \\
\\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [1. \text{投诉目的}]: \text{As one of your consumers, it is with great reluctance for me to lodge a} \\
& \text{complaint about } \underline{\hspace{5cm}} \text{ (e.g., the phone bought yesterday).} \\
\\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [2. \text{过渡句}]: \text{Relevant reasons for my complaint go as follows.} \\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [3. \text{原因一：质量缺陷}]: \text{First, the product has serious flaws. Specifically, } \underline{\hspace{4cm}} \\
& \text{(e.g., the screen has scratches / the food is stale).} \\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [4. \text{原因二：服务态度}]: \text{Second, although I tried to negotiate, your staff seemed} \\
& \text{reluctant to help me out, which brought me great inconvenience.} \\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [5. \text{提出诉求}]: \text{In order to compensate for the losses incurred, I am wondering if it is} \\
& \text{possible to } \textit{refund me / substitute the flawed one} \text{.} \\
\\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [6. \text{结尾重申}]: \text{Once again, I hope my problems can be well solved.} \\
\\
& \text{Best Wishes,} \\
& \text{Li Ming}
\end{aligned}
$$

## 4. 道歉信 (Apology Letter)

**适用场景：** 遗失物品、迟到、未能赴约、损坏借物。

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \text{Dear } \underline{\hspace{4cm}}, \\
\\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [1. \text{道歉目的}]: \text{I am writing to express my sincere apology to you for } \underline{\hspace{4cm}} \\
& \text{(e.g., losing your book / missing the appointment).} \\
\\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [2. \text{解释原因(过去时)}]: \text{Due to the fact that } \underline{\hspace{5cm}} \text{ (原因: I was in a} \\
& \text{hurry / careless), I } \underline{\hspace{4cm}} \text{ (错误行为), which might bring about} \\
& \text{great inconvenience to you.} \\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [3. \text{补救措施}]: \text{In order to make up for the losses, I will } \underline{\hspace{4cm}} \text{ (方案:} \\
& \text{buy a brand-new one / pay for it).} \\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [4. \text{请求原谅}]: \text{You must be dismayed about what I have done, but I promise} \\
& \text{similar incidents will not occur.} \\
\\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [5. \text{结尾再次抱歉}]: \text{Hope you can understand my dilemma. Once again, sorry for the trouble.} \\
\\
& \text{Yours,} \\
& \text{Li Ming}
\end{aligned}
$$

## 5. 推荐信 (Recommendation Letter)

**适用场景：** 推荐景点（地标/文化）、书籍（情节）、电影（票房/立意）。

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \text{Dear } \underline{\hspace{4cm}}, \\
\\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [1. \text{推荐目的}]: \text{I am writing to } \textit{recommend / hand-pick} \text{ } \underline{\hspace{4cm}} \text{ (推荐项)} \\
& \text{to you as the best choice.} \\
\\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [2. \text{过渡句}]: \text{Relevant reasons for my recommendation go as follows.} \\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [3. \text{理由一：地位/热度}]: \text{First, it is one of the } \textit{landmarks / best-sellers} \text{ in} \\
& \underline{\hspace{3cm}} \text{ (地点/领域).} \\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [4. \text{理由二：内容/文化}]: \text{Second, it boasts a profound history and marvellous culture.} \\
& \textit{(电影书籍备选: The plot is full of ups and downs.)} \\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [5. \text{理由三：主观感受}]: \text{Third, when you } \textit{wander in it / read it} \text{, your comprehension} \\
& \text{about Chinese culture will be further deepened.} \\
\\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [6. \text{结尾期待}]: \text{Once again, I hope you can accept my recommendation.} \\
\\
& \text{Yours,} \\
& \text{Li Ming}
\end{aligned}
$$

## 6. 求职信 (Job Application Letter)

**适用场景：** 申请志愿者、学生会职位、实习生。**（侧重个人能力展示）**

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \text{Dear Sir or Madam,} \\
\\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [1. \text{求职意向}]: \text{I am writing with a hope that you could offer me an opportunity of} \\
& \text{being } \textit{recruited / employed} \text{ as } \underline{\hspace{4cm}} \text{ (职位名称).} \\
\\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [2. \text{过渡句}]: \text{Relevant qualifications about myself go as follows.} \\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [3. \text{优势一：语言证书}]: \text{First, my } \underline{\hspace{3cm}} \text{ certificate ensures fluent communication} \\
& \text{with foreigners.} \\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [4. \text{优势二：性格特点}]: \text{Second, my enthusiasm, patience and teamwork skills} \\
& \text{make smooth collaboration available.} \\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [5. \text{优势三：经验能力}]: \text{Third, my internship at } \underline{\hspace{3cm}} \text{ equipped me} \\
& \text{to efficiently resolve } \textit{thorny and nagging} \text{ problems.} \\
\\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [6. \text{结尾期待}]: \text{I will be rather appreciative if you could give me a chance. Looking} \\
& \text{forward to your early reply.} \\
\\
& \text{Yours,} \\
& \text{Li Ming}
\end{aligned}
$$

## 7. 招募通知 (Notice / Call for Volunteers)

**适用场景：** 以组织名义招募志愿者、通知活动详情。**（侧重对应聘者的要求）**

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \hspace{6cm} \textbf{Notice} \\
& \hspace{10cm} \text{May 5th, 2025} \\
\\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [1. \text{背景与目的}]: \text{A } \underline{\hspace{4cm}} \text{ (活动名) will be held next month in our university.} \\
& \text{We hope to take advantage of this opportunity to } \underline{\hspace{3cm}} \text{ (活动意义).} \\
& \text{Therefore, we would like to } \textit{recruit volunteers / introduce the activities} \text{.} \\
\\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [2. \text{过渡句}]: \text{Relevant details/requirements go as follows.} \\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [3. \text{要求一：语言能力}]: \text{First, volunteers should be } \textit{adept at / proficient in} \\
& \text{English, spoken English in particular.} \\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [4. \text{要求二：性格要求}]: \text{Second, they are supposed to be enthusiastic and patient} \\
& \text{since there are many thorny problems to be dealt with.} \\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [5. \text{要求三：经验优先}]: \text{Third, the } \textit{experienced / veterans} \text{ will enjoy the priority.} \\
\\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [6. \text{联系方式}]: \text{Please send your application to our email address } \underline{\hspace{3cm}} \\
& \text{if you are interested. Do not hesitate to reach us if you have further questions.} \\
\\
& \text{Postgraduate Association}
\end{aligned}
$$

## 8. 感谢信 (Letter of Gratitude)

**适用场景：** 感谢帮助、感谢款待、感谢指导。

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \text{Dear } \underline{\hspace{4cm}}, \\
\\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [1. \text{感谢目的}]: \text{I am writing to express my sincere thanks to you for } \underline{\hspace{4cm}} \\
& \text{(e.g., your hospitality / your generous help).} \\
\\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [2. \text{表达情感}]: \text{I can still recall how considerate and wonderful you are.} \\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [3. \text{细节一：具体帮助}]: \text{First, your warm help made it possible for me to } \underline{\hspace{4cm}} \\
& \text{(结果: e.g., finish the task).} \\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [4. \text{细节二：强调重要性}]: \text{Second, had it not been for your assistance, I would} \\
& \text{have been at a loss about what to do.} \\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [5. \text{想要回报}]: \text{In order to repay what you have done, I would like to } \underline{\hspace{4cm}} \\
& \text{(e.g., mail some specialties / treat you a meal) to deepen our relationship.} \\
\\
& \hspace{0.5cm} [6. \text{结尾再次感谢}]: \text{Once again, I hope you can accept my thanks.} \\
\\
& \text{Yours,} \\
& \text{Li Ming}
\end{aligned}
$$

### 1. 邀请信 (Invitation Letter)

**适用场景**：邀请外教、同学、专家参加比赛、会议或典礼。

**Dear [被邀请人姓名/职位],**

[首段：发出邀请]

It is with great honor for me to invite you to [attend / participate in / be a judge for] the [活动名称, 如: English Speech Contest].

[次段：活动详情]

Relevant details go as follows.

First, this [活动名称] will be held at [具体时间, 如: 2 p.m.] on [日期, 如: May 5th] in [地点, 如: the Student Center]. We hope to take advantage of this opportunity to [活动目的, 如: cultivate students' interest].

Second, by then, there will be about [数字] students and teachers attending this activity.

Third, [补充细节, 如: some gifts will be available / it will be an online livestreaming].

[尾段：期待出席]

I will be rather appreciative if you could spare time to join us. Looking forward to your early reply at your earliest convenience.

Yours,

**Li Ming**

---

### 2. 建议信 (Suggestion Letter)

**适用场景**：给图书馆、朋友、学弟学妹提供建议。

**Dear [收信人],**

[首段：表明身份+目的]

As a [身份, 如: student in our college / close friend], I am writing to offer you some conducive suggestions on [建议的主题, 如: improving services / learning English].

[次段：具体建议 (HOW + WHAT)]

Relevant suggestions go as follows.

First, you should [建议动作1] so as to [达到的效果1]. For example, it would be a sound policy for you to [具体做法, 如: download an App].

Second, it would be a must for you to [建议动作2], which can help you [达到的效果2].

Third, you had better [建议动作3] to [达到的效果3].

[尾段：期待采纳]

Once again, I hope you can accept my tips. Please feel free to contact me if you have further questions.

Yours,

Li Ming

---

### 3. 投诉信 (Complaint Letter)

**适用场景**：投诉产品质量（电子产品/食品）或服务态度。

**Dear Sir or Madam,**

[首段：投诉目的]

As one of your customers, it is with great reluctance for me to lodge a complaint about the [投诉对象, 如: mobile phone I bought / bad service].

[次段：问题描述+后果]

Relevant reasons for my complaint go as follows.

First, [问题细节1, 如: the screen has scratches / the food is stale].

Second, although I once tried to negotiate with your staff, he/she seemed to be reluctant to help me out, which brought me great inconvenience.

[尾段：提出诉求]

In order to make up for the losses incurred, I am wondering if it is possible to [解决方案, 如: substitute the flawed one / refund me]. Once again, I hope my problems can be well solved.

Yours,

Li Ming

---

### 4. 道歉信 (Apology Letter)

**适用场景**：因遗失物品、迟到、未能赴约而道歉。

**Dear [收信人],**

[首段：真诚道歉]

I am writing to express my sincere apology to you for [道歉的事由, 如: losing your book / missing the appointment].

[次段：解释原因+补救措施]

Due to the fact that [解释原因, 如: I was in a hurry / I was careless], I [发生了什么错误], which might bring about great inconvenience to you.

In order to make up for the losses incurred, I am wondering if it is possible to [补救方案, 如: buy a brand-new one for you / treat you a meal].

[尾段：再次抱歉]

You must be dismayed about what I have done, but I promise similar incidents will not occur. Hope you can understand my dilemma.

Yours,

Li Ming

---

### 5. 推荐信 (Recommendation Letter)

**适用场景**：推荐旅游景点、书籍、电影。

**Dear [收信人],**

[首段：推荐目的]

I am writing to recommend [推荐对象名称] to you as the best choice.

[次段：推荐理由]

Relevant reasons for my recommendations go as follows.

First, it is [地位/特点, 如: one of the landmarks in Beijing / a best-seller].

Second, it boasts a profound history and marvellous culture. (注：若是电影书刊，可改为: The plot is full of ups and downs.)

Third, when you [动作, 如: wander in it / read it], your comprehension about Chinese culture will be further deepened.

[尾段：祝福]

Once again, I hope you can accept my recommendation.

Yours,

Li Ming

---

### 6. 求职信 (Job Application Letter)

**适用场景**：申请志愿者、实习生或兼职工作。

**Dear Sir or Madam,**

[首段：求职意向]

I am writing with a hope that you could offer me an opportunity of being recruited as [职位名称, 如: a volunteer / an assistant].

[次段：个人优势]

Relevant qualifications about myself go as follows.

First, my [证书/能力, 如: CET-6 certificate] ensures fluent communication with foreigners.

Second, my enthusiasm, patience and teamwork skills make smooth collaboration available.

Third, my previous experience equipped me to efficiently resolve thorny problems.

[尾段：期待面试]

I will be rather appreciative if you could give me a chance. Looking forward to your early reply.

Yours,

Li Ming

---

### 7. 感谢信 (Letter of Gratitude)

**适用场景**：感谢老师指导、朋友帮助或某人的款待。

**Dear [收信人],**

[首段：表达谢意]

I am writing to express my sincere thanks to you for [对方做的具体好事, 如: your hospitality / your help].

[次段：感激细节]

I can still recall how considerate and wonderful you are.

First, your warm and generous help made it possible for me to [结果, 如: finish the task].

Second, had it not been for your assistance, I would have been at a loss about what to do.

[尾段：回报]

In order to repay what you have done, I would like to [回报方式, 如: mail some specialties to you] to deepen our relationship.

Yours,

Li Ming

---

### 8. 通知 (Notice)

**适用场景**：招募志愿者、通知活动举办。

**Notice**

**[日期]** May 5th, 2025

[第一段：背景+目的]

A [活动名称] will be held next month in our university. We hope to take advantage of this opportunity to [活动目的]. Therefore, we would like to [通知目的, 如: recruit volunteers / introduce the activities].

[第二段：具体要求/细节]

Relevant [qualifications / details] go as follows.

First, [要求1/细节1, 如: volunteers should be adept at English].

Second, [要求2/细节2, 如: the experienced will enjoy the priority].

[第三段：联系方式]

Please send your application to our email address 123@test.com if you are interested. Do not hesitate to reach us if you have further questions.

[署名：组织名称]

**Postgraduate Association**
